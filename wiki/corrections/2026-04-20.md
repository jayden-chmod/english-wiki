### Motics 3rd Round Interview Practice — Harvinder 1:1 + Salinna/James opener

| What I wrote | Correction | Reason | Related |
|---|---|---|---|
| Let go | Let's go | "Let's" = "let us" — missing 's | — |
| how can I improvement | how I can improve | Embedded question: no inversion; "improvement" is a noun — need verb | [[noun-verb-confusion]] [[embedded-question-word-order]] |
| the one of the most impressive | one of the most impactful | Remove "the"; "impressive" ≠ "impactful" | [[impressive-vs-impactful]] |
| contributes to society | contribute to society | Parallel structure: "improve... and contribute" | — |
| they give us a lot of explainability | they provide strong explainability | "provide" more precise than "give us" in formal context | — |
| I'll be get used to it | I'll get used to it | No "be" before "get used to" | [[false-auxiliary-errors]] |
| that's why choose healthcare | that's why I chose healthcare | Missing subject pronoun "I" | [[missing-subject-pronoun]] |
| everything is the same | I can adapt to any domain | Vague; rephrase directly | — |
| a a really | a really | Repeated article — pause instead of repeating | — |
| we can easily expect the outcome | we can predict the outcome reliably | "predict reliably" more precise; "easily expect" too casual | — |
| we also introduce | we should also introduce | Missing modal — proposing, not stating fact | — |
| every system can fall into failures | every system can fail | Simpler and more natural | — |
| vicvious cycliccycle | virtuous cycle | Pronunciation/clarity — slow down | [[virtuous-cycle]] |
| Amazon says it as flywheel | Amazon calls it a flywheel | "calls it" not "says it as" | — |
| those data will be our asset | that data becomes our asset | Data = singular in business English; subject-verb agreement | [[subject-verb-agreement-errors]] |
| I will say sorry for patient | I'd first express concern for the patient | Conditional "would" for hypothetical; "express concern for" not "say sorry for" | — |
| it depends on my position | it depends on the context — legally and contractually, that's a different conversation | Clarify what "position" means | — |
| we made processes that final decision is verified | we build processes where the final decision is always verified | "build" (present habit); "where" not "that" | — |
| I don't understand why it is pointing out to us | I'd question why that responsibility falls on the engineer | Stronger, more professional framing | — |
| when I use just gemini, gpt, or claude | from working with Gemini, GPT, and Claude directly | More natural phrasing for describing experience | — |
| they are influenced by surroundings | they're sensitive to surrounding context | More precise technical phrasing | — |
| it was natural decision | it was a natural decision | Missing article "a" | [[missing-article]] |
| separate context as much as I can | keep each context as focused as possible | More precise; "focused" better than "separated" | — |
| if the model become powerful enough | if models become powerful enough | Subject-verb agreement | [[subject-verb-agreement-errors]] |
| This approach may become useless | this approach may become less necessary | "useless" too strong and self-defeating | — |
