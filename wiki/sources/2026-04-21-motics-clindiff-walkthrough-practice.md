# Motics Clindiff Walkthrough Practice — Session 04

## Summary

First rehearsal of the in-person clindiff walkthrough for the Motics 3rd round interview (Block 1). **Feedback-only mode**: Jayden narrates each demo section solo, AI returns utterance feedback only — no roleplay character replies, no pushback questions.

Covered three of roughly seven Loom-script sections: opening, Generate screen, navigation to the Compare screen.

## Session Breakdown

**Turn 1 — Opening**
- Self-introduction + demo framing + open-interrupt invitation.
- Main issues: register (contractions "gonna", casual "guys"), number mismatch in open invitation ("a question" → "any questions").

**Turn 2 — Generate screen**
- Described upload affordance, model selection, intent to surface failure cases.
- Main issues: missing articles (×2: "small model", "dropdowns"), awkward "with + expecting" construction, noun repetition instead of pronoun.

**Turn 3 — Navigation to Compare screen**
- Described clicking Generate and transitioning to the compare screen.
- Main issues: missing article on "generate button", unnatural "it navigates" (app as subject of `navigate`).

## Key Corrections: 10 total

Major patterns this session:
- **Missing articles** (×3): "a/the generation screen", "set **the** small model", "from **the** dropdowns", "hit **the** generate button" (or proper-noun form "hit Generate")
- **Informal register in interview context** (×2): "gonna" → "going to", "guys" → "all / everyone"
- **Awkward `with + -ing` for intent** (×1): "set model with expecting to fail" → "pick the model, expecting it to fail"
- **`navigate` collocation** (×1): "it navigates to the compare screen" → "it takes us to the compare screen"
- **Singular/plural in open invitations** (×1): "if you have a question" → "if you have any questions"
- **Typos** (×2): "queation", "woth" — not tracked as mistake patterns
