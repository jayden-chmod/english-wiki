# Polite Requests

## Meaning
Ways to ask for something politely in English. Using the right level of politeness is essential — especially with strangers, in shops/cafés, and at work.

## Ordering at a Café/Restaurant

### Casual-Polite (most common)
- **"I'll have a latte."** — simple and natural
- **"Can I get a grande?"** — casual but polite
- **"I'd like a cappuccino, please."** — slightly more formal

### More Formal
- **"Could I have a latte, please?"**
- **"May I have a latte?"**

### Too Direct (avoid)
- ❌ "Give me a latte." — sounds rude to staff
- ❌ "I want a latte." — grammatically fine but blunt

## General Polite Requests
- **"Could you...?"** — "Could you pass the salt?"
- **"Would you mind...?"** — "Would you mind closing the window?"
- **"Do you think you could...?"** — "Do you think you could help me with this?"
