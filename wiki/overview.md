## 🎯 Areas for Improvement
- **🔥 Missing articles** (now high severity): specific UI elements always take `the`; singular count nouns take `a/an`. Highest-signal pattern for demo narration ([[missing-articles]])
- **Register in interview context**: avoid `gonna`, `guys`, `kinda`; standard contractions (`I'll`, `we're`) are fine ([[informal-register-in-interview]])
- **Subject-verb agreement**: watch singular subjects ("it", "the model", "data") → need -s on verb ([[subject-verb-agreement-errors]])
- **Noun/verb form**: after modals/infinitives, use verb base form — not noun: "how I can **improve**" not "improvement" ([[noun-verb-confusion]])
- **Word choice**: "impressive" ≠ "impactful" — use [[impactful]] for real-world effect
- **Embedded questions**: "how **I can**" not "how **can I**" inside a statement ([[embedded-question-word-order]])
- **UI-narration collocation**: apps `take` you to a screen; they don't `navigate` ([[take-you-to]])
