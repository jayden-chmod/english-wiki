# Café Ordering

## Useful Expressions

### Ordering
- "I'll have a [drink]." → most natural way to order
- "Can I get a [drink]?" → casual-polite
- "I'd like a [drink], please." → more formal
- See also: [[polite-requests]]

### Size
- "What size would you like?" (staff asks)
- "A grande, please." / "Medium, please."

### Asking Price
- "How much is it?" / "How much does it cost?"
- See also: [[question-formation]]

### Payment
- "I'll pay with card." / "Card, please."
- "Cash or card?" (staff asks)

### Staff Responses
- "Coming right up!" — preparing your order immediately
- "That'll be $X." — stating the price
- "Would you like anything else?" — asking about additional orders
