# Café Ordering Roleplay

## Context
Practicing ordering at a café in English.

## Conversation

**Me:** I want to ordering a latte please.

**AI Feedback:** ❌ "want to ordering" → ✅ "want to order" — Use the base form of the verb after "to" (to-infinitive).

**AI Response:** Sure! What size would you like?

---

**Me:** Give me a grande size.

**AI Feedback:** ❌ "Give me" sounds too direct/rude → ✅ "I'll have a grande" / "Can I get a grande?" — Use polite request forms when ordering.

**AI Response:** A grande latte coming right up! Would you like anything else?

---

**Me:** No, that's it. How much is it cost?

**AI Feedback:** ❌ "How much is it cost?" → ✅ "How much does it cost?" / "How much is it?" — Don't mix "is" and "cost" together. Use either "How much is it?" or "How much does it cost?"

**AI Response:** That'll be $5.50. Cash or card?

---

**Me:** I'll pay with card. Thank you so much!

**AI Feedback:** ✅ Perfect! Natural and polite.

**AI Response:** You're welcome! Have a great day!
